The UCJEPS E-loan Portal builds on top of the CollectionSpace collection management system used by the UC and Jepson Herbaria at the University of California, Berkeley, and the public search portal developed to access those collections. It provides an easy way for herbaria partners and patrons to access electronic loans of specimen images and data.

To request an E-loan, please contact Andrew S. Doran, andrewdoran@berkeley.edu.

This program was written by Rick Jaffe (rjaffe@berkeley.edu), John Lowe (jblowe@berkeley.edu), and Amy Wieliczka (amywieliczka@berkeley.edu) of the Research Information Technology group within Information Services & Technology (IS&T) at the University of California, Berkeley.

Comments, suggestions, bug reports, and so on are most welcome.

This program is written in Python using Django, jQuery, and Backbone.js accessing a Solr4 datasource. The data is extracted and updated nightly from the UCJEPS CollectionSpace system.
