Welcome!

This is the gateway to electronic loans of herbarium specimens provided by The University and Jepson Herbaria, University of California, Berkeley.
To arrange for a loan, please contact Andrew S. Doran, andrewdoran@berkeley.edu.

To search all of the herbaria's specimens, see the Public Search portal. For type specimen lists and images, see Type Specimens at UC/JEPS.
For more California vascular plant records, visit The Consortium of California Herbaria for specimen data from 30 contributing institutions. For taxonomic treatments and dichotomous keys of California plants, see The Jepson eFlora (for vascular plants), or The California Moss eFlora.
